Perk Miner Launches Revolutionary No-Risk Advertising Platform That Puts Privacy First and Guarantees a Minimum 900% ROI for Business Advertisers

New platform connects consumers and businesses through secure messaging — with zero fees unless a sale is made (a pay-per-sale / pay-per-close model), and zero compromise of member data.

Las Vegas, NV — July 1, 2026 — Perk Miner (PerkMiner.com) today announced the launch of its groundbreaking advertising and rewards platform that fundamentally re-imagines the relationship between consumers and businesses. Built on the principles of privacy, transparency, and performance-based advertising, Perk Miner delivers a first-of-its-kind model where members pay nothing—ever—and business advertisers only pay after they make a sale.

A Paradigm Shift in Advertising

Founded by a business owner who experienced firsthand the frustration of spending thousands on leads, pay-per-click campaigns, email marketing, and SEO with little to no return, Perk Miner was built to solve a problem that millions of small business owners face every day: paying for advertising with no guarantee of results.

“I have paid thousands for leads, thousands for PPC campaigns, and a lot in SEO without making sales,” said the founder of Perk Miner. “My system is a complete No-Risk, No-Loss-Marketing model — advertisers pay zero if they don’t make a sale or close a deal. It’s a win for members and a win for advertisers.”

How It Works for Members

  • Perk Miner membership is completely free — no subscription fees, no contracts, no commitments, and no hidden fees of any kind.
  • Members browse participating business advertisers and connect through secure, one-on-one messaging.

Critically, Perk Miner:

  • Never sells or shares member contact information
  • Never tracks browsing history to serve targeted ads
  • Never uses listening or surveillance to generate spam or push marketing
  • Gives members full control over when and to whom they disclose their contact information

When a member selects a business, that connection is exclusive—leads are not sold to multiple competing businesses. Members are protected from harassment, unsolicited calls, and the data exploitation that is standard practice on most consumer platforms.

Members who make a purchase through Perk Miner earn 2% cash back on purchases up to $2,500 — paid directly by Perk Miner from the advertising fee, not by the business advertiser.

How It Works for Business Advertisers

  • Business advertisers join Perk Miner with no membership fees, no contracts, and no commitments.
  • No cost for leads, phone calls, appointments, or meet-ups.
  • Advertisers are only charged a 10% advertising fee, capped at $250, and only after a sale is made or a deal is closed.

Key benefits for business advertisers include:

  • Guaranteed minimum 900% ROI on advertising spend
  • Minimum 10:1 Marketing ROI ratio—paying $1 or less for every $10 in sales generated (excluding Cost of Goods Sold)
  • Real-time analytics dashboard: gross sales, advertising fee paid, net gross, ROI, and Marketing ROI ratio
  • 1% cash back on up to $2,500 of the sale when finalizing a transaction, rewarding timely processing (equals 10% of the ad fee paid)
  • Immediate payment — businesses are paid directly by the customer at the time of sale; Perk Miner does not collect, hold, or deduct fees from business payments.

Transaction finalization is simple: once the business has been paid by the member, they scan the member’s QR code and submit the total amount paid, or complete the transaction manually for remote sales. (Businesses should keep enough funds in their account balance to cover advertising fees, starting with $25 which allows up to $250 in sales.)

Privacy by Design

At its core, Perk Miner is a privacy-first platform. Unlike most consumer-facing advertising platforms that monetize user data, track behavior, and sell contact information to the highest bidder, Perk Miner is structurally designed to prevent those practices. Member contact information is never used as a commodity.

“We protect member privacy and they decide who and when to disclose their contact information to businesses,” the founder explained. “Perk Miner gives the member total control and complete protection.”

Cash Back and Referral Commissions

All member cash back (2%), member referral commissions (varied at tiers 2-5), business cash back (1%) and business referral cash back (varied at tiers 2-5) are paid by Perk Miner out of the advertising fee collected.

  • Business advertisers are never asked to fund these rewards—they are an investment by Perk Miner
  • Members also earn a commission for referring businesses to advertise (1%, plus varied for tiers 2-5)

About Perk Miner LLC

PerkMiner.com is a consumer and business advertising platform built to eliminate risk, waste, and privacy violations that define traditional digital advertising. By connecting members and businesses through secure, one-on-one messaging and charging advertisers only upon completed sales, Perk Miner delivers guaranteed results for businesses and guaranteed privacy for consumers.

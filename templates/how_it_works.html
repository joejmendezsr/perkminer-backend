How It Works For All PerkMiner Transactions:

1 - Member searches for and visits or contacts a Business of their choice.

First Option (Member physically visits the advertiser):


2A - Member notifies the Business that they're a Perk Miner Member before paying for the purchase.
2B - Member pays the Business directly for their purchase. ONCE PAID, TRANSACTION MUST BE FINALIZED.
2C - Member clicks on "Show QR Code." Member QR Code is displayed. Member shows the QR Code to the Business.
2D - Business Owner or Staff Member is notified that they need to scan a QR code (Member’s name is shown).
2E - The "Scan Payment QR code for (name)" button appears in the Business Dashboard (Business may have to refresh the page).
2F - Business clicks on the "Scan Payment QR code for (name)" button, scans the QR Code, enters total paid by Member, then clicks "Finalize".
2G - Business ad fee is deducted from Account Balance (Business must have enough in their Account Balance to cover the ad fee).
2H - Both the Member and Business are notified that the transaction is recorded and see their instant cash back.
2I - Member clicks on "End session", then the Business ends session. Both can view earnings in their dashboard.

Second Option (Member Requests Service with secure messaging):


3A - Member clicks on the "Request Service" button. A new session begins between the Member and Business (Member's info protected).
3B - Member selects service type, enters description of service requested, budget, and then submits the request.
3C - Business is notified by email that their Business has been selected and reviews the service request in their dashboard.
3D - Both the Member and the Business click on "My Active Sessions" from their dashboard to view all active requests.
3E - Both the Member and Business select each other to start secure messaging — Member's privacy is protected until Member is ready to share contact info.
3F1 - If Member decides not to proceed, they can "End Session" without sharing any further information (Business does not have access to Member's contact information).
3F2 - If Business decides not to proceed, they can "End Session" without any penalty and without incurring any fees (Business does not pay anything for unfinalized leads).
3F3 - If Member decides to continue, they can share address/phone details with the Business (Member decides what information they want to give).
3G - Business can set appointments or schedule a service call with the Member without incurring any fees, unless the Business charges for service calls. If a Member pays any fees, THE TRANSACTION MUST BE FINALIZED.
3H - Business can send a quote before or after a visit (Quote includes a description of the service and amount) to be viewed by the Member for that active session only - Member must refresh the page to view the quote).
3I1 - If Member decides not to proceed or accept the terms of the quote, they can "End Session" without a penalty. Business may also end the session without incurring any costs, unless the Member pays any amount.
3I2 - If Member agrees with the quote, they click "Accept & Pay" (QR appears at the top of the page) and pays the business directly for the service. ONCE PAID, THE TRANSACTION MUST BE FINALIZED.
3J - Member shows the QR code to the Business so they can scan the QR code. Business can also finalize the transaction remotely.
3K - Business is notified they have a QR code to scan or they may finalize the transaction manually from their dashboard (Business may have to refresh the page).
3L - Business clicks on "Scan QR to Finalize Payment" from their active session page, scans QR code or manually finalizes, enters total paid by Member and click on "Finalize".
3M - Business ad fee is deducted from Account Balance (Business must have enough in their Account Balance to cover the ad fee).
3N - Both the Member and Business are notified that the transaction is recorded and see their instant cash back.
3O - Member clicks on "End session", then Business ends session. Both can view earnings in their dashboard.

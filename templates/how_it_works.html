Lifecycle of a PerkMiner Transaction

1 Member searches for and visits or contacts a business of their choice.
2 If visiting physically, the member pays the business directly for their purchase.
3 Member clicks "Show QR Code," notifies the business, and displays their membership QR code.
4 Business Owner or Staff is notified they need to scan a QR code (member’s name is shown).
5 Business or staff scans the QR code, enters total paid by member, then clicks "Finalize".
6 Both member and business are notified that the transaction is recorded and see their cash back.
7 Member ends session, then business ends session. Both can view earnings in their dashboard.
8 If member clicks "Request Service," a new session begins between member and business (member info protected).
9 Member selects service, enters description, budget, and submits request.
10 Business is notified and can review the service request on their dashboard and by email.
11 Business clicks "My Active Sessions" from dashboard to view the request and start secure messaging.
12 Both member and business securely message—privacy is protected until member is ready to share contact info.
13 If member doesn’t wish to proceed, they can "End Session" without sharing further contact info.
14 If member agrees, they can share address/phone directly with the business.
15 Business can charge an in-person visit fee if needed; fee amount is finalized just like a purchase.
16 Business can send a quote before or after visit (description and amount visible to member).
17 If member doesn’t wish to proceed with quote, they can "End Session"—no further fees.
18 If member agrees with the quote, they click "Accept & Pay" and pay for the service.
19 Member then shows QR code to the business for finalization, or business finalizes remotely.
20 Business is notified they have a QR code (or manual finalization request) waiting.
21 Business scans QR code or manually finalizes, enters total paid, and clicks "Finalize".
22 Transaction is recorded; both parties see their cash back.
23 Both parties "End Session" and earnings appear in dashboard.

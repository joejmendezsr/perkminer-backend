How It Works For All PerkMiner Transactions:

1 - Member searches for and visits or contacts a business of their choice.
2A - If visiting physically, the member pays the business directly for their purchase. TRANSACTION MUST BE FINALIZED.
2B - Member clicks "Show QR Code," notifies the business, and displays their transaction QR code.
2C - Business Owner or Staff is notified that they need to scan a QR code (member’s name is shown).
2D - Business or staff scans the QR code, enters total paid by member, then clicks "Finalize". Business ad fee charged.
2E - Both member and business are notified that the transaction is recorded and see their instant cash back.
2F - Member clicks on "End session", then business ends session. Both can view earnings in their dashboard.
3A - If member clicks "Request Service," a new session begins between member and business (member info protected).
3B - Member selects service, enters description, budget, and submits request.
3C - Business is notified by email and can review the service request in their dashboard.
3D - Both the Member and the Business click on "My Active Sessions" from their dashboard to view all active requests.
3E - Both the member and business select each other to start secure messaging — member privacy is protected until member is ready to share contact info.
3F1 - If member doesn’t wish to proceed, they can "End Session" without sharing further contact info (Business cannot access member details).
3F2 - If member agrees to continue, they can share address/phone directly with the business (member decides what information they want to give).
3G - Business can set appointments or schedule a service call with the member (If the business charges for service calls, TRANSACTION MUST BE FINALIZED).
3H - Business can send a quote before or after a visit (description of service and amount) to be viewed by the member for that active session only).
3I1 - If member doesn’t wish to proceed with quote, they can "End Session"—no further fees. Business may also end session without any cost incurred.
3I2 - If member agrees with the quote, they click "Accept & Pay" and pay the business directly for the service. TRANSACTION MUST BE FINALIZED.
3J - Member then shows QR code to the business for finalization, or business may finalize the transaction remotely.
3K - Business is notified they have a QR code to scan or finalize manually.
3L - Business scans QR code or manually finalizes, enters total paid, and clicks "Finalize". Business ad fee charged.
3M - Transaction is recorded and both parties see their instant cash back earned.
3N - Both parties "End Session" and both parties can view their earnings in their dashboard.

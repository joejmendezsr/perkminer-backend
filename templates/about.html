We believe in member privacy (we don't treat our members like leads), honest rewards, real results for businesses, and a trusted digital marketplace for everyone to enjoy.

Joe Mendez, Founder
Joe is passionate about building honest, results-driven connections between members and businesses, with privacy as a top priority. Drawing on decades of experience in business, tech, and advertising agencies, he knows the frustration of wasted ad dollars (paying for leads, PPC, SEO, etc. and getting Zero Sales or having ad fees that drastically reduce your profit margins).

As a consumer, Joe understands what's it's like having your personal data sold to others as a lead (non-stop phone calls, your mail-box getting filled with spam emails, etc.). That's why Joe incorporated secure messaging as a way for members and businesses to connect without ever comprimising a member's contact information.

In 2007, Joe created a Pay-Per-Sale/Pay-Per-Close model to help businesses achieve meaningful results without high costs—earning a program copyright in 2008. With PerkMiner, he flipped the script: businesses earn cashback from making sales, not purchases, enjoying a minimum 900% ROI (paying $1 or less in fees for every $10 sold - max $250), plus cash back.

Joe knows real WORD-OF-MOUTH is the best advertising. That’s why PerkMiner pays out up to 87.5% of its ad revenue to reward members and business owners who spread the word and complete sales—so everyone wins.

Joe is also a U.S. Navy (naval intelligence) and law enforcement (police sergeant) veteran. PerkMiner is his vision for helping businesses grow—with trust and transparency at the center.

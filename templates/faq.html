Frequently Asked Questions

← Back to Home
  • What is PerkMiner?
    PerkMiner is a platform that connects consumers and businesses through secure messaging, offering rewards for members and guaranteed ROI for business advertisers.
  • How much does it cost to join PerkMiner?
    It’s free for members—no subscription fees, no contracts, and no hidden costs!
  • How are businesses charged?
    Business advertisers only pay a 10% fee (capped at $250) after a completed sale. There are no upfront payments for leads, calls, or clicks.
  • Is my information safe?
    Yes. PerkMiner never sells, shares, or exploits member data. You control when and to whom you share your contact info.
  • Can I earn rewards?
    Absolutely! Members get 2% cash back on purchases up to $2,500, paid by PerkMiner—not the business.
  • Where can I get help?
    Contact support any time at support@perkminer.com.
